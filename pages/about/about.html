Sabores del Sur is a casual and intimate Spanish-inspired restaurant, specializing in fresh, handmade pasta and family-style dishes designed to share.

With the warm hospitality and energy of a traditional Italian home, we offer family-style service: all dishes are designed for sharing and the food arrives in the middle of the table, as it is prepared. The antipasti arrives first, with pasta courses close behind. We also offer house-made gluten-free options available daily. All menu items are available for dine-in or take-out.






Tarta de Santiago

Each decadent slice is a celebration of simplicity and elegance, paying homage to centuries-old recipes passed down through generations. Indulge in a slice of history and savor the timeless allure of Tarta de Santiago, a culinary masterpiece that transcends time and borders.

Sangria

Crafted by blending robust red wine with a medley of luscious fruits such as oranges, lemons, and berries, this refreshing concoction boasts a tantalizing harmony of sweet and tangy flavors. Infused with a hint of citrusy zest and the subtle richness of brandy, each glass of Red Sangria is a sensory journey, evoking images of sun-kissed vineyards and lively fiestas. Whether enjoyed leisurely on a sunny terrace or served alongside a spread of tapas, this iconic Spanish drink captures the convivial spirit of gatherings and celebrates the joy of shared moments

Paela Mixta

The rice, cooked to a delectable golden hue, absorbs the rich flavors of the ingredients, while aromatic spices such as paprika and garlic add depth to every mouthful. Served in a traditional shallow pan, Paella Mixta is a feast for the senses, embodying the spirit of Spanish gastronomy and bringing people together in celebration of good food and camaraderie.
